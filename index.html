How does Purrfect Pair work?

Lifestyle assessment

You can’t judge a cat by it’s fur (trust us, we’ve tried). That’s why we ask a few questions about your lifestyle, what you want in your cat, and other factors that impact your decision.

Cat-matching software

Your lifestyle responses are paired with a growing inventory of cats with information on personalities, health concerns, compatibility with other pets, and other details that help your begin a fruitful, mutually happy decision.
